Deconsecrate – “A Fatal Encounter”

It is fair to say that Belgium is one hell of a breeding ground for metal bands. Talented people come together to form a large and wide culture of underground metal music. One of these bands is Deconsecrate. Founded in 2012, in the H8000 area, they are a combination of metalcore, hardcore, black and death metal.

This mix feels best in their latest track, “A Fatal Encounter”. The song, together with the video, are just a small sample of everything that represents the sphere of Belgian metal. Personally, I like the three minutes of unstopped violent lyrics very much. Everyone in it played a role in conveying their feelings, the one with the sprinkler being my favorite.

If you want to know the Belgian underground music scene better, I invite you to listen to Deconsecrate and all those like them.

Review by Nicoleta Mironescu

7 January 2022
